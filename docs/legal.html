Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Q-base ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email and identity verification via Firebase Authentication.
  • User-Generated Content: Encrypted clinical questions, shared collections, and AI prompts.

B. Automatic Data Collection

  • Usage Analytics: Interaction metrics for AI performance and token quota management.
  • System Logs: Crash reporting for application stability.

2. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE)

Q-base implements a zero-knowledge architecture. Neither our staff nor our cloud providers possess the keys required to decrypt your private messages or medical data.

All interpersonal communication is encrypted locally using AES-GCM-256. Symmetric keys are protected via the hardware-backed Android Keystore.

3. Data Storage & Infrastructure

We leverage world-class infrastructure to ensure data integrity and privacy:

  • Firebase (Google Cloud): Global real-time synchronization for metadata and identity.
  • Appwrite (Sydney Region): Specialized regional blob storage for high-performance ZIP collection sharing (syd.cloud.appwrite.io).
  • AI Core (Gemini & Groq): Intelligence provided by Google Gemini and GroqCloud. Your data is transmitted over TLS and never used for training third-party models.

Terms of Service

1. Moderation & Safety

While we value absolute privacy, we maintain a strict policy against abuse and harassment.

The "Report and Forward" Mechanism

Because of our E2EE architecture, we cannot proactively scan messages. We rely on user-initiated reporting:

  • Users can report specific messages for moderation.
  • The reporting device generates a decrypted fragment of the conversation for manual review.
  • This fragment is sent with cryptographic signatures to verify the origin of the content.

2. Account Enforcement

Violations of our safety policies result in account-level actions:

  • Banning: Banned accounts are flagged globally across the network.
  • Local Hiding: Your app automatically filters out communication from accounts flagged with an isBanned status to maintain a safe environment.

3. Intellectual Property

Users retain ownership of their medical collections but must ensure they have legal rights to share any copyrighted diagrams or texts. Q-base complies with valid DMCA requests by restricting access at the account level.
