Qbase Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Our Mission

At Qbase, we empower learners through collective intelligence and AI-driven study tools. Privacy is at the core of our platform architecture, designed to give you control over your educational journey.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to operate, provide, and improve our services.

Information You Provide

  • Account Data: Mobile phone number and profile identifiers.
  • Educational Content: Questions, collections, and AI prompts created by you.
  • Communications: Messages exchanged with peers to foster collaborative learning.
  • Connected Contacts: Optional address book access to find and connect with fellow students.

Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Logs: Service-related diagnostic and performance reports.
  • Device Metrics: Hardware model, OS version, and app-specific identifiers.
  • General Location: IP-based city/country estimation for security and context.

2. Encryption & Security (E2EE)

Q-base implements state-of-the-art End-to-End Encryption to ensure your private data remains yours alone.

  • Person-to-Person Chats: Powered by ECIES (Google Tink). Your messages are encrypted with the recipient's public key; only their private key, stored securely in the Android Keystore, can decrypt them.
  • Group Chats: We utilize Client-Side Fan-out encryption. Every message is individually encrypted for each group member, ensuring perfect forward secrecy even as group membership evolves.
  • Collection Sharing: A hybrid AES256-GCM and 25519-based encryption model. Shared study materials are encrypted with a unique symmetric key, which is itself transmitted via an E2EE channel.

3. How We Use Information

Subject to your choices and applicable law, we use information to:

  • Personalize your study sessions and collective knowledge flows.
  • Research and develop new AI features to assist your learning.
  • Maintain the safety and integrity of the Qbase ecosystem.
  • Ensure technical reliability and respond to support requests.

4. AI Interactions & Data

When you utilize AI assistance within Qbase, your prompts are processed to generate highly relevant educational content. We partner with state-of-the-art AI providers including Google Gemini and Groq, ensuring that all data processing adheres to enterprise-grade security standards.

Note: You can always manage your data sharing preferences in the app settings.

5. Information Sharing

Knowledge is meant to be shared, but your privacy remains protected.

  • Public/Shared Profile: Your name and shared collections are visible to your chosen learning circles.
  • Infrastructure Partners: We utilize Google Firebase and Appwrite for secure data hosting and synchronization.
  • Legal Disclosures: Information may be shared if required to comply with a legal obligation or to prevent harm.

6. Your Controls

You have full authority over your data on Qbase:

  • Manage Profile: Update your identity and "about" section at any time.
  • Data Portability: Export your knowledge base and session history.
  • Account Deletion: Permanently remove your presence from our primary infrastructure.
